Privacy Policy

By signing up for a PyroNeon account, you agree to this privacy policy.

We (PyroNeon Software) may log or store some of the following information from our users: IP Addresses (Network addresses), usernames, email addresses, user-generated content, and API requests/responses.

We will not sell your information like email addresses, ip addresses, or other data to third parties like advertisers or email spammers. Your privacy is very important to us.

By logging in to your account, you are agreeing to let us store and use cookies on your device. We will only access cookies that were created by us (PyroNeon Software).

Sensitive data like account passwords are kept confidential. In fact, your raw password is never stored on our database, but rather a cryptographic hash (using the bcrypt algorithm) of it.

We will not publish your information to the public. However, in the event of illegal use of our services, we may send account information to law enforcement to aid in an investigation.
