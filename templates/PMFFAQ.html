Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms can I play on?

Desktop, Android, and Web versions are currently planned.
However, currently, only Desktop is supported. Any desktop
that can run Java should be able to support the game.

Do I download the .JAR or .EXE?

The Jar file is for computers with Java installed.
If you're running Windows, you can install the .EXE file.
There isn't a difference, but the EXE doesn't require
Java to be installed on your computer.

I found a bug in the game!

Please let us know! Fill out this anonymous form
about your experiences! We are always looking
to patch bugs. Your reports will help make
the game more enjoyable for everyone!
(If you choose to submit your email, we promise never to share it with anyone else.)

Is it free to play?

Puzzle Minefield will always be free to play.
In the future, there may be optional cosmetics or other
bonus features for sale. On some platforms, you may
have the choice to watch ads for in-game rewards.

How do I make an account?

Currently, as of the Alpha release, accounts
are not implemented. There are no experience
or ELO metrics to track, and no official online
matchmaking servers.
