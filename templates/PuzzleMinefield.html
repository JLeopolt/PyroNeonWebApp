About

Puzzle Minefield is a classic puzzle solving game,
with enhancements such as cross-platform gameplay,
and online progress tracking, but chiefly: Competitive Multiplayer
gameplay. Play head to head with others in random matches for ranks
and to climb the leaderboard, or host your own server and invite
friends/strangers to play with you, through LAN or online gameplay.

It's easy to learn how to play! There are only 3 different controls
you need to know, Left Click, Right Click, and Middle Click.
Enjoy playing through and honing your skills by competing against
others online. Although it's easy to learn, it's difficult to master
with increasingly skilled players facing you and more difficult maps
to solve.

Enjoy the custom graphics and sound design, with interchangeable
resource packs available, pick your favorite skin and enjoy a whole new
experience playing with it.
