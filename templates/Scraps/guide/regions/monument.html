Monument Regions

Last Updated: {{ last_modified }}

This article is about region permissions, not Monuments in general.

Monument Regions are unique regions that protect monument structures from being destroyed or built in. When a player is within a monument region, they will see a purple region indicator, instead of a green or red Lockbox region indicator.

  • Players can't build, break, or destroy blocks with explosives/fire in a monument region.
  • Players can open doors and chests in monument regions.
  • Players can still PvP and use weapons with impunity in a monument region.
  • Monument regions do NOT extend the entire world height, but rather a spherical radius.
  • Lockboxes cannot be placed within range of a monument region.
